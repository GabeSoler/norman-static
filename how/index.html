How It Works

Getting Started

Initial Consultation

We begin with a free 20-minute phone call where you can tell me what's bringing you to therapy and ask any questions you have. This helps us both determine if we're a good fit to work together. First Session

If we decide to proceed, we'll arrange an initial in-person session at my practice in Edinburgh (89 Causewayside, EH9 1QS). This first meeting is for assessment and to agree on how we'll work together. We'll discuss your goals, my approach, and establish a formal contract. Ongoing Sessions

Regular sessions are 50 minutes long and take place weekly at the same day and time. This consistency is important for building the therapeutic relationship and creating meaningful change. After your first 10 in-person sessions, online sessions via secure video call become available if that better suits your circumstances. If scheduling allow it is possible to adjust the frequency to twice-weekly or fortnightly, depending on what serves your needs best. Reviews and Progress

We'll review our work together every 10 sessions to make sure the direction and approach remain aligned with your goals. You can also request a review at any point if something feels important to discuss about how therapy is going.

What to Expect

Therapy is a collaborative process. You can expect me to be fully present, attentive, and working to understand your experience. I'll guide you through practices that help you notice and work with feelings and sensations, always checking in to ensure you feel safe and supported. Our work involves building skills in managing difficult emotions before moving into deeper or more challenging territory. Everything will proceed collaboratively to ensure the intensity of the work is manageable for you. Practical Information
